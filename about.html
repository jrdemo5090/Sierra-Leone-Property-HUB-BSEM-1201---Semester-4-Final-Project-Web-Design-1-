About Salone Property Hub

Empowering Sierra Leone Through Secure Digital Property Solutions

Since 2022, Salone Property Hub has revolutionized the way Sierra Leoneans buy, sell, and verify land and properties. Our mission is to create a transparent, secure digital ecosystem that democratizes access to property services across all 16 districts.

100% Verified Transactions

Every property is thoroughly verified against government land records, ownership databases, and legal documentation to ensure complete transparency, authenticity, and buyer confidence before any transaction takes place.

Bank-Level Security

Advanced encryption, secure authentication systems, and modern cybersecurity protocols safeguard your personal information, property records, and digital transactions around the clock.

National Reach

Serving all 16 districts of Sierra Leone, we connect buyers, sellers, investors, surveyors, and institutions through one trusted nationwide property platform.
