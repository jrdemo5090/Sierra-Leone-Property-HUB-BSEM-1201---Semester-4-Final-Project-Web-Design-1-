Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about contacting us and our services.

What are your customer service hours?

We're available Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. For urgent matters, call our after-hours emergency line.

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. Urgent matters are prioritized.

Can I reach you outside business hours?

Yes, you can email us anytime at support@sph.gov.sl or call our emergency line for urgent matters at +232 76 999 999.

How do I report a problem or issue?

Contact our support team via phone, email, or our contact form. We'll investigate promptly and provide a solution.
